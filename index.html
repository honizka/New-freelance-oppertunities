Understanding the Earning Potential

The €300K Reality Check

The target of €300,000 annually represents a significant leap from standard freelance earnings in Germany. Even experienced specialists in high-demand fields like Consulting & Management or SAP earn €160,000-€170,000 gross annually, assuming 1,380 billable hours per year.

Earning Scenarios Breakdown

  • €100/hour: 3,000 billable hours needed (unsustainable)
  • €217/hour: 1,380 billable hours needed (premium rate)
  • Standard Range: €80-€142/hour for top fields

Remote Work and Legal Considerations

German Freelance Regulations

Registration

Register with Finanzamt (tax office) as Freiberufler

VAT (Umsatzsteuer)

Mandatory for €300K target (above €22K threshold)

Tax Payments

Quarterly advance payments (Einkommensteuervorauszahlungen)

International Client Considerations

  • Income generally taxable in Germany if resident
  • Double taxation agreements may apply
  • B2B services outside EU: 0% VAT (with conditions)
  • EU clients: reverse charge mechanism

No-Investment Startup
